# Figure 3

**Figure 3. Independent \(\Lambda_{\mathrm{hold},v}\) verification performance across residual-risk predictors and deterministic descriptors.**
Performance was evaluated against the independent held-out residual-risk target \(u_{\Lambda_{\mathrm{hold},v}}\). (a) Top-10% AUPRC measured high-risk localisation, with higher values indicating better identification of the highest residual-risk regions. (b) MAE measured residual-risk scale agreement, with lower values indicating better agreement with the independent target. (c) Spearman correlation measured rank agreement across the evaluated spatial support, with higher values indicating better preservation of residual-risk ordering. Points show mean independent-test performance and whiskers show 95% bootstrap confidence intervals. The selected A4 CNN and Full CNN are shown separately from direct transfer baselines and deterministic descriptors. AUPRC = area under the precision-recall curve; MAE = mean absolute error.
