# Figure 4

**Figure 4. Residual-risk error structure across independent target levels.**
Absolute residual-risk error was computed relative to the independent held-out target \(u_{\Lambda_{\mathrm{hold},v}}\). (a) Empirical cumulative distributions of absolute residual-risk error summarised the fraction of evaluated pixels below each error value. Curves shifted toward the left indicated smaller errors. (b) Mean absolute residual-risk error was summarised across deciles of the independent target, showing how each method behaved from low-risk to high-risk regions. (c) Boxplots summarised absolute error within the top residual-risk decile, where accurate localisation and scale agreement are most important for residual-risk verification. Deciles were defined using the independent held-out target, not predictor outputs. The comparison included the A4 CNN, Full CNN, gain envelope, reconstruction magnitude, image gradient, and intervention magnitude descriptors.
